Front End Web Development

A Medium For Communication

Alfonso Ruiz

Web Developer & Student Udacity

Fascinated by the internet, and a bit of foodie.

I've never really thought about front-end development as a medium of communication until I my intructor spoke about it today. I always thought of it as just code, nothing more nothing less.

But when you frame it as a medium communication well it's pretty darn important.If we can't commumnicate our message through our UI in a logical, clear and orginazied way our users may not even stick around to see what else we have regardless of how good it is.

Its a reminder that there is no one part of software development that's more important than another. As we learn to craft systems rather than pages, we must invest effort into shaping these often missed states of design and create with a component lifecycle that can support everyone.
